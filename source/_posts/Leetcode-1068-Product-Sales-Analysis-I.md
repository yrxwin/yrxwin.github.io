#### 解题思路
这题解法非常直接，两个表用`join`做外连接即可
#### 示例代码 (mysql)
```sql
SELECT p.product_name, s.year, s.price
FROM Sales AS s
JOIN Product AS p ON s.product_id = p.product_id
```
